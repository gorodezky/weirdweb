ABOUT US

Weirdwolf Designs is a clothing company founded in June of 2016 by Jose Zaga and Dan Gorodezky. The two-man team divided the responsibilities of taking inventory, keeping track of sales, and making decisions regarding pricing from the get-go. Sales launched in the summer of 2016 and business has steadily grown since. The company hopes to continue making trendy shirts and expanding to other items.
